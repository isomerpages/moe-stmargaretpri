### **P6 Science Learning Journey to Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve 2026**

##### 7 Jan 2026

Our Primary Six students embarked on an exciting adventure to Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve, a sanctuary teeming with diverse wildlife. 
This interactive learning journey transformed the mangrove forest into a living classroom, allowing the students to deeply connect with nature and study how local plants and animals adapt to their environment. 
Keeping a sharp lookout, the eager children spotted monitor lizards roaming the area, silently gliding snakes, sleeping bats hanging upside down, alongside various birds and fish. 

More than just an ordinary science lesson, it was a captivating firsthand encounter with the wonders of the natural world!
